About Me

Dr. George Dumitru was born and raised in Toronto, Ontario. After high school, he attended the University of Toronto where he graduated with a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc). After that, he packed his bags and went off to Boston University where he graduated with a Doctor of Dental Surgery.

Once he graduated, he headed straight home to practice dentistry among his family, friends and neighbours – to serve his community.

Dr. George Dumitru strives to improve your oral health with effective family and cosmetic dentistry procedures. His dental solutions include crowns, bridges, implants, root canals and more. He also offers teeth whitening services and regular checkups. Book your dentist appointment with Dr. Dumitru today. New patients are welcome, and Saturday and evening appointments are available.

Giving Back

While Dr. George has made it his practice to provide friendly and professional dental care to families in Toronto for more than 20 years, he is also actively involved around the community. His clinic has been instrumental in supporting and establishing a dental school in Ethiopia. His goal was to provide education so that families there could someday have the dental care that we enjoy in Canada.
